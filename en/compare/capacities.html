The difference in one paragraph

Capacities is cloud-first by design, which is exactly why its AI can only work on what you're willing to upload. The sensitive, high-value work — the very stuff AI could help with most — stays off-limits. Fount inverts that: files on your disk, AI on your device, so the AI can see everything precisely because none of it leaves.
