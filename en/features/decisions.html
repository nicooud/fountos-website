The objection

"Who ever reads this back?" You will — the day someone asks "why did we do it this way?" Decisions keeps the choice and its reasoning together, so the answer is one search away.

How it works

Note it. Keep the why.

01

Note the choice where you make it.

In the meeting note, the message, the journal — wherever it happens.

02

It keeps choice and reason together.

Decisions gathers what you decided and why, not just the outcome.

03

Answerable later.

"Why did we pick this?" has an answer, with the note it came from.

The detail that earns trust

The reasoning stays attached.

Every decision links back to the note where you made it — so a year from now, the why is still with the what, in a file you own.
