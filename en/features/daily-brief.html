Daily Brief

Three things matter today. Here's why.

Not a wall of widgets — three things, chosen from everything you've written, each with the reason it rose to the top today.

Join the waitlist
The objection

"Not another dashboard." It's the opposite of a dashboard. No widgets, no forty tiles, no red badges. Three things, and the reason each one made the list today.

How it works

Read everything. Show you three.

01

It reads what you already wrote.

Commitments, notes, and decisions across your folder — nothing you have to enter twice.

02

It picks the few that matter.

Three things for today, chosen from everything, so you're not the one triaging at 8am.

03

It shows its reasoning.

Each item says why it made the list, and links to the note it came from.

The detail that earns trust

Three things, not thirty.

The Brief is deliberately small. It would rather show you too little and be trusted than show you everything and be ignored.
