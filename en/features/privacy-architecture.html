Privacy architecture

If we wanted to read your files, we couldn't.

No upload, no sync to us, no server that holds your work. The AI runs on your device by default. That's not a policy — it's the architecture.

Join the waitlist
The objection

"My data in someone else's cloud." There's no cloud in the loop. No upload, no sync to us, no server that holds your work. We can't read your files because they never reach us.

How it works

On your disk. On your device.

01

Your files stay on your device.

Plain markdown in your folder — there's no Fount server and no hidden copy.

02

The AI runs on your device.

On-device by default. Turn off the internet and Fount keeps working.

03

Off-device is opt-in, per use.

If you ever connect an external model, it's your account, your choice, one request at a time — and Fount tells you what it would send.

The detail that earns trust

Not a policy. The architecture.

No telemetry, no analytics, and no training on your work. If we wanted to read your files, we couldn't — and that's the point. Built in the EU by a privacy-first founder. Read the full privacy policy →
